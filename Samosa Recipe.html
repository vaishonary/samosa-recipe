Samosa Recipe

Ingredients:

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups maida or flour
  • 4 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water (as needed)

For the Filling:

  • 2 large boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1/2 cup green peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste (swad anusar)
  • 1 tbsp oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix flour, salt and oil. Slowly add water to make a stiff dough. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, then green peas and mashed potatoes.
  3. Add all spices and salt. Cook for 5â€“7 minutes. Let it cool.
  4. Roll the dough into circles, cut in half, and shape into cones.
  5. Fill each cone with the filling (masala) and seal the edges with water.
  6. Deep fry in medium-hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve hot with mint chutney or tamarind sauce!
